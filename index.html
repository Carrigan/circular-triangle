Who We Are
Circular Triangle is a cross-industry, interdisciplinary working group unlocking the potential of a circular economy in the Triangle. We do this by bringing together leaders across industries and sectors to create a shared vision for circular economy initiatives; by pushing local governments to fund circular economy waste assessments; and advocating for policies favorable to circular economy strategies.
By 2050, two-thirds of us will live in cities
Urban centers are grappling with the effects of our current take-make-waste economy
Cities consume over 75% of natural resources, produce over 50% of global waste, and emit between 60-80% of greenhouse gases
What if we...
Design out waste and pollution from cities?
Keep products and materials in use in cities and maintain their value?
Regenerate natural systems in and around cities?
